An AI agent that lets you ask business questions in plain English and get instant charts and insights from 3.4 million e-commerce orders.

**Example:**
```
You: "What are the top 10 most ordered products?"
System: [Generates SQL → Executes → Shows bar chart] Done in 2 seconds!
```
